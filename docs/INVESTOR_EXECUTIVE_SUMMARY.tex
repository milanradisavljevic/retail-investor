\section*{Executive Summary}
Intrinsic is a deterministic equity analysis platform with three priorities:
\begin{itemize}
  \item transparent scoring logic instead of black-box models,
  \item reproducible backtesting with clear strategy controls,
  \item regime-aware capital allocation in changing market conditions.
\end{itemize}

All major outputs are traceable to explicit formulas and fixed rules. The full mathematical reference is available in \texttt{docs/CALCULATION\_REFERENCE.tex} and \texttt{docs/CALCULATION\_REFERENCE.md}.

\section*{Method in One Page}
\textbf{Scoring model (four pillars):}
\[
Score = w_V \cdot Valuation + w_Q \cdot Quality + w_T \cdot Technical + w_R \cdot Risk
\]
with deterministic preset-specific weights.

\textbf{Regime model (macro overlay):}
\[
R_{comp} = 0.35\,S_{VIX} + 0.30\,S_{YieldCurve} + 0.20\,S_{Fed} + 0.15\,S_{CPI}
\]
plus a hard override to \texttt{CRISIS} when market stress is extreme (e.g., high VIX).

\textbf{Capital adaptation:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \texttt{RISK\_ON}: full investment.
  \item \texttt{NEUTRAL}: full investment, base profile.
  \item \texttt{RISK\_OFF}: reduced equity fraction, increased defensive weight.
  \item \texttt{CRISIS}: strongest cash buffer plus stricter quality threshold.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Why This Is Credible}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Deterministic}: same inputs produce the same outputs.
  \item \textbf{Auditable}: each score and filter has a documented formula.
  \item \textbf{Configurable}: presets and risk overlays are explicit, versioned, and testable.
  \item \textbf{Practical}: results are presented as actionable dashboards, not just raw research data.
\end{itemize}
